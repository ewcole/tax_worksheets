Welcome to the Tax Worksheet Page!

Like many people, I use a spreadsheet to hold my work while I fill out my tax forms. Over the years, I've spent more and more time on them, and it seemed good to share them with anyone who wants them.

Please be aware that these are not official tax forms. You should not attempt to file your tax return with them.

NOTICE:

By downloading the spreadsheets, you acknowledge that you are using them at your own risk, and that there are no warranties, express or implied. I am not a tax expert and you should not ask me questions about tax law. I will not go to jail for you.
